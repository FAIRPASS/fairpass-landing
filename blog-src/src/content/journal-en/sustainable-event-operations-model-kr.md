## 🏷️ Why Does the Badge Matter? — The Smallest Touchpoint With the Biggest Impact

A name badge might seem like a minor operational detail. In reality, it is the **central infrastructure through which your entire event flow runs**.

### How Badges Create Operational Bottlenecks

- **Pre-event preparation**: Finalise attendee lists → Print badges → Assemble plastic holders and lanyards → Sort and lay out by session or category
- **On-site congestion**: Registration desks become chokepoints, requiring additional staff just to manage queues
- **Last-minute changes**: Walk-ins and name corrections cause delays and manual rework on the day
- **Post-event waste**: Dozens — sometimes hundreds — of plastic badge holders go directly to landfill

Each event cycle compounds the same costs: **time, budget, and environmental impact**.

### How FAIRPASS Redesigns the Entire Flow

FAIRPASS combines **eco-friendly paper badges + automated self-print kiosks + an integrated data management system** to fundamentally restructure how events are run.

- ✅ **Minimal pre-event preparation** — No badge assembly or manual sorting required
- ✅ **On-site automation** — Attendees print their own badges at self-service kiosks
- ✅ **Real-time flexibility** — Walk-ins and last-minute changes are handled instantly
- ✅ **Zero plastic** — 100% paper-based badge structure reduces plastic waste by **90%**
