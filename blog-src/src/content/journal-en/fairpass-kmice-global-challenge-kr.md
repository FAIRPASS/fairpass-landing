**FAIRPASS**, the innovative B2B event management platform developed by Blueorigin Co., has been selected for the **Korea Tourism Organization (KTO)'s 2024 Tourism Global Challenge** — a prestigious government-backed acceleration program designed to strengthen the global competitiveness of Korea's tourism and MICE industry.

---

## What Is the KTO 2024 Tourism Global Challenge?

The **2024 Tourism Global Challenge** is a support initiative organized by the **Korea Tourism Organization (KTO)**, established to foster sustainable growth and accelerate the global expansion of Korean tourism and MICE companies. Selected companies receive a comprehensive package of institutional support, market access opportunities, and resources to scale internationally. The program's primary goal is to identify and nurture innovation-driven companies capable of leading the next chapter of the Korean tourism industry on the world stage.

FAIRPASS's selection into this program marks a significant milestone — recognizing the platform's potential not just within Korea, but as a globally relevant solution for modern event organizers.

---

## Built on 20+ Years of MICE Industry Experience

FAIRPASS was developed by **Jeon Seo-yoon, CEO of Blueorigin**, who brings over **two decades of hands-on experience** in the MICE industry to the product. That deep operational knowledge is reflected in how FAIRPASS is designed: it addresses the real pain points that event planners and PCO agencies deal with on every project — from managing registrations to printing and distributing badges on-site.

Rather than building a generic SaaS tool, the team engineered FAIRPASS specifically around the workflows of professional conference and event organizers, making it immediately practical for corporate events, international congresses, trade fairs, and institutional summits alike.

---

## How FAIRPASS Transforms Event Registration and Badging

At its core, FAIRPASS **fully automates the end-to-end registration process** — from attendee sign-up and payment collection through to badge generation and access control. This eliminates the manual, labor-intensive workflows that have long been the norm in event operations.

Key operational benefits include:

- **Dramatic time savings:** Tasks that previously took **1–2 months** — including registration intake, attendee data management, and badge preparation — can now be completed **in under one hour**
- **Custom badge design:** Event-specific branding, session access tiers, and personalized attendee information can be incorporated into badge templates without additional production overhead
- **Print-on-demand badging:** Badges are printed only as needed, eliminating pre-printed surplus and reducing per-event costs
- **Reduced staffing requirements:** Automation replaces much of the manual coordination typically required from registration staff

For event managers juggling tight timelines and multiple stakeholders, this level of operational efficiency is transformative.

## Eco-Friendly Event Operations: FAIRPASS and ESG

One of FAIRPASS's most distinctive contributions to the MICE industry is its strong **environmental sustainability** profile. By minimizing paper consumption through digital-first workflows and on-demand badge printing, FAIRPASS helps organizations significantly reduce the material waste generated by traditional event registration systems.

This aligns directly with the growing ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) commitments of corporate event hosts and international organizations worldwide. As sustainability reporting becomes a standard expectation for large-scale events — particularly among multinational corporations, intergovernmental bodies, and academic institutions — adopting platforms like FAIRPASS is both a practical and reputational advantage.

FAIRPASS's eco-friendly approach:
- Minimizes unnecessary paper and plastic badge usage
- Reduces print waste by issuing only what is needed
- Supports organizations in meeting their own ESG and sustainability targets
- Enhances the public image of events as responsible, forward-thinking gatherings
