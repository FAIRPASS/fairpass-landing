The Korea MICE Association, with government backing in 2022, evaluated 83 companies that developed MICE technology applicable to exhibitions, conventions, and large-scale events — and selected **6 outstanding companies** as **DX Excellence MICE Enterprises**, recognising those with the strongest commercialisation potential.

### FAIRPASS Recognised for Its All-in-One Event Registration & Access Management Solution

**BlueOrigin**, the company behind **FAIRPASS**, secured its place on the prestigious list by demonstrating end-to-end event operations capabilities — covering **online event registration**, payment processing, **badge issuance**, and **real-time access control**, all within a single unified platform.

What sets FAIRPASS apart is its **automated, unmanned badge issuance system** — replacing traditional plastic or vinyl lanyards with on-demand **paper badge printing** at self-service kiosks. Within just **6 months of launch**, FAIRPASS had already been deployed at multiple large-scale events, including:

- 🏛️ **Smart Land Expo** (스마트 국토 엑스포)
- 🤝 **Korea–Spain Business Forum** (한·스페인 비즈니스 포럼)

These deployments demonstrated FAIRPASS's reliability and scalability in demanding, high-footfall event environments.

> 💡 **FAIRPASS** is a B2B event management platform that streamlines the entire attendee journey — from **QR code-based check-in** and **automated event registration** to **on-site kiosk badge printing** — reducing staff workload and eliminating queues at registration desks.
