## 🏷️ Why the Badge? — The Smallest Touchpoint with the Biggest Impact

A badge might seem like an afterthought. In reality, it is **the central infrastructure through which your entire on-site operation flows**.

### How the Traditional Badge Process Creates Operational Drag

- **Pre-event preparation**: Finalise attendee list → Print badges → Assemble plastic holders and lanyards → Sort and stage for distribution
- **On-site congestion**: Registration queues grow, staff are stretched thin
- **Last-minute chaos**: Walk-ins and changes cause delays and errors
- **Post-event waste**: Boxes of plastic holders head straight to landfill

Each time this cycle repeats, the **cumulative cost — financial, operational, and environmental — compounds**.

### How FAIRPASS Redesigns This from the Ground Up

FAIRPASS combines **eco-friendly paper badges + automated self-issue kiosks + an integrated data management system** to fundamentally rethink how event registration works.

- ✅ **Minimal pre-event prep** — No badge assembly or pre-sorting required
- ✅ **On-site automation** — Attendees self-issue badges directly at the kiosk
- ✅ **Real-time flexibility** — Walk-ins and last-minute changes handled instantly
- ✅ **Plastic-free operation** — 100% paper construction reduces badge waste by **up to 90%**
